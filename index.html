Hello.

I'm Amy.
I'm a web developer
and I care about stories.

Over the years, I've spent time writing reading designing advising listening coding editing . . .

. . . shaping and telling
my story or someone else's
to expose its heart,
to help it be received as intended.

I believe the stories told by websites should be
accessible by everyone,
delightful to take in,
and clearly understood.

I built the Wordbook App
because I believe educational resources should be widely accessible
and precisely wielded words make self-expression possible.

I created this website and am actively refining it
to record and cement what I've been learning
and to share my story.
