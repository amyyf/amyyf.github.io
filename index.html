Hello.

I'm Amy.
I'm a software developer
and I care about stories.

Over the years, I've also been a writer editor designer counselor crafter and creator . . .

. . . shaping my stories or others'
to tell them clearly.

I believe the stories told by websites should be
accessible by everyone,
delightful to take in,
and presented intentionally.

I've worked with TypeScript, React, and Less to help build Sun Life Onboard.

I worked on a large NYC media site redesign,
creating front-end UI elements and a design system while at Alley.

I've also enjoyed working with Next.js, headless CMSs, Storybook, Node, and Jest.

I built the Wordbook App
because I believe educational resources should be widely accessible
and precisely wielded words make self-expression possible.

I created this website and will continue refining it
to record and cement what I've been learning
and to share my story.
