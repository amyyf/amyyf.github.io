writing
reading

crafting and telling my story or someone else's

accessible by everyone,
delightful to take in,
fast,

because I believe educational resources should be widely available,
and words wielded precisely make self-expression possible.

I created this website and am currently crafting and refining it
as a place to record and cement what I've been learning
and to share my story.
